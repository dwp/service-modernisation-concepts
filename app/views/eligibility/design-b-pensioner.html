Based on your circumstances, you might be able to get these benefits and financial support.

Pension Credit

Estimated payout time: 3 weeks

Pension Credit gives you extra money to help with your living costs if you're over State Pension age and on a low income. To get Pension Credit you must be receiving State Pension and be receiving certain benefits. Find out more about Pension Credit.

Funeral Expense Payments

Estimated payout time: 3 weeks

Funeral Expenses Payment can help with the costs of a funeral, such as burial or cremation fees. To get Funeral Expenses Payment you must be receiving certain benefits, be a close relative of the deceased and organising a funeral in the UK. Find out more about Funeral Expenses Payment.

You can choose to apply for some or all of the benefits you are eligible for at the same time.

If you decide to apply for more than one benefit, you will be asked for all of the information and evidence needed to complete your application.
