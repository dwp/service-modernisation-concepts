Pension Credit

Estimated payout time: 2 weeks

What is it?

Pension Credit tops up your State Pension. To get Pension Credit you must be receiving State Pension but not getting the full amount. You must also be receiving certan benefits. Find out more about Pension Credit.

Funeral Expenses Payment

Estimated payout time: 3 weeks

What is it?

Funeral Expenses Payment can help with the costs of a funeral, such as burial or cremation fees. To get Funeral Expenses Payment you must be receiving certain benefits, be a close relative of the desceased and organising a funeral in the UK. Find out more about Funeral Expenses Payment.

You can choose to apply for some or all of the benefits you are eligible for at the same time.

If you decide to apply for more than one benefit, you will be asked for all of the information and evidence needed to complete your application.
