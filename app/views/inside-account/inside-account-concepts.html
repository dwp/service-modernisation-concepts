The screens in this prototype explore the concept of 'Inside the account'. See 'Inside the account' in Confluence (opens in new page) for for further background.

The prototype

Although the pages are connected in the prototype, the pages do not form a comprehensive journey. Each page should be viewed as standalone.

The assumption is that the user would log in via OneLogin before arriving at the account home page. See further work on assumptions in Confluence (opens in new tab).
