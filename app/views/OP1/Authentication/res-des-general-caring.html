Design of this prototype

General limitations

Next steps

  • Identify user needs of people reporting breaks in care. Work with other teams – The Carer’s Allowance team is currently conducting research on breaks in care.
  • Base the questions asked on signed off decision trees to incorporate them in the user journey.
  • Explore opportunities to avoid the customer having to manually enter data, for example by including an address picker or by pulling data that DWP already has access to.
