Tell DWP about a change page

This page explains research and design specific to the Tell DWP about a change page. This is a key page in the journey as it's the page the user lands on after logging in.

What was the design intention?

  • The intention for the design of this page was to explore a concept of a change of circumstance service landing page.
  • Areas of the page included personal information and links off to change of cirucmstance groupings.
  • Groupings for changes are based on the Universal Credit 'Report a change' journey.

Considerations for further research and design work

  • There are a lot of options for the customer on this page (change details in the top table, report a change in the bottom half of the page or switch profile). We suggest simplifying this page.
  • Conduct more research to understand how people group different changes of circumstances, possibly through a card sorting exercise.
  • Conduct desk research to gather groupings used by other DWP or government services when reporting a change.
  • Carry out desk research on existing government and DWP design patterns for pages like this that display customer personal details.
